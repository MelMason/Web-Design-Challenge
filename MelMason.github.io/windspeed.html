Wind Speed


This graph compares the wind speed to latitude. We can see that the wind speed at this time of year is fairly low. Most of the wind speeds at this time are less than 20 miles per hour across the range of latitudes. The Northern Hemisphere had slightly higher wind speeds, reaching a max of about 36 miles per hour.
