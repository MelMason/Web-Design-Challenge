Humidity


This graph compares the humidity to latitude. It appears that there are a cluster of locations that have 100% humidity at this time of year. These locations are closer the equator, and continue into the Northern Hemisphere. Interestingly enough, the Northern Hemisphere appears to have a greater amount of locations with less than 60% humidity when compared to the Southern Hemisphere. Perhaps this is due to the seasons occuring in each of the hemispheres.
